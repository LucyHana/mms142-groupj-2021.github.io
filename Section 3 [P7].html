What is its status now?

Users of digital wallets have boomed due to the pandemic, with the Philippines specifically showing sustained growth in digital wallet users beginning from 2020. Both markets of consumers and merchants have been opting to provide and use digital wallet services to be able to adhere to regulations involving less physical contact. Also, with the rise of e-commerce especially in the pandemic, digital wallets have complimented its services.
