What was there before Digital Wallets?

The dominant way of transaction before the e-wallets were in banks, wherein teller transactions were the most common if not the only convenient way during those times. Back then it would take hours before you could proceed with your withdrawal or deposits. The teller would require the customer to provide authentic identification before a transaction could be placed whereas now, we can rely with our passwords and different authentication applications (two-way authentication processes). Bank teller transactions may be tedious and/or stressful, but there is a sense of better security with your hard-earned money back then.
